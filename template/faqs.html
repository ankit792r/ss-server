Frequently Asked Questions

What is KeyDrop?

KeyDrop is a one-time text sharing platform that allows users to send and retrieve text using a secure 6-digit key.

Is my data stored permanently?

No. Your data is deleted immediately after it is accessed once.

Do I need an account?

No signup or login is required. KeyDrop is completely anonymous.

How secure is it?

Each key is unique and temporary. Combined with one-time access, this ensures high security.

Can I share large text?

Yes, but there may be limits depending on backend implementation.

What happens if I lose the key?

Unfortunately, without the key, the data cannot be retrieved.

Can I use this on mobile?

Yes! KeyDrop is fully responsive and works seamlessly across devices.
