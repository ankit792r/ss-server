About KeyDrop

KeyDrop was created to solve a simple but important problem — securely sharing text without leaving traces. Traditional messaging apps store your data, while email and cloud platforms are often too slow or complicated.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a fast, secure, and anonymous way to share temporary data using one-time access keys.

Use Cases

  • Sharing passwords securely
  • Sending API keys or tokens
  • Quick clipboard transfer between devices
  • Sharing confidential notes
  • Developers exchanging code snippets

How It Works

When you submit text, KeyDrop generates a unique 6-digit key. Share this key with your recipient. Once accessed, the text is deleted permanently — ensuring one-time visibility.

Privacy First

We do not store your data longer than necessary. Everything is designed with privacy at its core.
